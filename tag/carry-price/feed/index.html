It was more like a gigglefest between two teen-age girls who got carried away with some hair pulling. Because although all hell broke loose between Montreal and the Bruins with Timmy skating down to fight Price, it had to be hard for Price to take him seriously when he was already laughing by the time he got to the other end. What on earth do you call that? I mean really? God bless Timmy because he is certainly a ray of sunshine – who else laughs and throws punches all at the same time!?

BTW – the penalty box had more players in it than a can of oysters or the actual bench for that matter. Not a sight you see every day.
